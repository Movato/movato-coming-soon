Privacy Policy for Movato's Facebook Login

At Movato, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you log in to our website using Facebook Login.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to Movato using Facebook, we may collect the following information:

  • Basic profile information: Name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Facebook User ID: To authenticate your account.
  • Other permissions you grant: Any additional information you choose to share when using Facebook Login, such as location or friends list (depending on your privacy settings on Facebook).

We only collect the data necessary to provide access to our website and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through Facebook Login to:

  • Verify your identity and authenticate your login.
  • Provide access to Movato features and services.
  • Enhance your user experience by personalizing content.
  • Communicate with you about your account or transactions on Movato.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Storage and Security

We store your information on secure servers, and we implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

4. Data Retrieval and Deletion

You have the right to access, retrieve, and request the deletion of any personal data we have collected from you.

  • Data Retrieval: If you request to retrieve your data, we will provide all the information associated with your account within a reasonable time frame.
  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account or any personal data we hold, please contact us at facebook@movato.com. Upon your request, we will permanently delete all of your information from our servers and notify you once the process is complete. Please note that data deletion is irreversible, and once deleted, it cannot be restored.
